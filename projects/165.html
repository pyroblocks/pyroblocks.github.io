How to Play

The objective of 165 is to draft an RLCS team capable of a perfect 165 point season. You are picking real players’ real seasons. The goal is the best-performing, most well-rounded roster possible. Success is decided by a non‑linear engine that grades your three players against every player at their position in their era.

1. The Roll

Each round, a slot machine assigns a random RLCS season (S1–2026) and either NA or EU. Draft the best available player into an open slot. Three rounds, one per roster spot.

2. The Roster

Three players total. On desktop, drag a placed player to another eligible slot to rearrange; on mobile, tap a player on the roster bar to move it.

3. The Skips

One Season re‑roll for the entire game.

4. Scoring

  • Era-relative rating (0–100): Each player is scored by their online-only statistical performance per game versus other players in their own era, so a S1 pro competes fairly with a 2026 one. All three starters are weighted into the rating, though better players are weighted more then worse ones. However, no single position can carry the team to 165.
  • All-around performance: Relative statistical performance is important among all stats, not just goals.
  • Non‑linear curve: Points rise with steep diminishing returns. Going undefeated is the hardest stretch by far.
